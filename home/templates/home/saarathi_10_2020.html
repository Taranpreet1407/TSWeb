Since the pandemic has approached in India, lot of things have changed including the Employment Rate which has also impacted the GDP which is the lowest in last 50 years i.e. -23.9%. Based on our market research and conversations with around 20,000 Jobseekers, students, working professionals and the corporate companies, we came to know that there has been a change in the corporate policies and the hiring structure. Thus, creating a confusion between jobseekers and the companies.

Thus, TalentServe has One-Stop simple solution where we have taken a Not For Profit Initiative to create the positive impact in Indian Economy by creating job across India. Here, the Jobseekers will be attending a Career Management Session. This session will help them clear their doubts regarding their career and the new normal opted by the companies. Once the session is over the Jobseekers will get an opportunity to attend 5 Job interviews with 5 different companies.

"TalentServe’s state-of-the-art algorithm will help you land with best curated job that suits your passion."
