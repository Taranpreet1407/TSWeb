Who is a Solopreneur?

Well, Solopreneur is a mixture of two words, Solo and Entrepreneur. It means that you are an entrepreneur that prefers to work alone or solo.This means that you can work with people, but you aren’t actually hiring employees with an office. You can hire contractors and simply pay by hour or project.

What's needed to be a Solopreneur at TalentServe?

Your business idea should be based on something you’re passionate about and can see yourself doing day in and day out for the foreseeable future.

  1. Candidates who are interested for HR Recruiting.
  2. No need of office
  3. No capital needed
  4. No budget needed

What does a Solopreneur have?

Solopreneurs are BYOB(Be Your Own Boss). They start and run their Entrepreneurial journey on their own from conceptualizing, planning & executing.

Qualities of a successful solopreneur:
  • Enthusiasm
  • Work Ethic
  • Flexibility
  • Creativity
  • Media savviness
  • Self-Reliance
  • Self-Confidence
  • Grit
  • People Skills
  • Passion for doing over earning
  • Management and Organizational skills

Eligibility

  • Should understand HR Business.
  • Wants to start a business with a less than limited capital.
  • Should understand work ethics and a business responsibilities.
  • Passionate enough to work for their dreams.
  • Number 1 Goal oriented.
  • Willin to work part time (or) as a side business.
  • Any fresher with Entrepreneurial mindset can apply too.

Benefits of being a Solopreneur

  • Flexibility of your working time and opportunity of working from Home.
  • Earn your desired income.(Know your capabilities to earn compete with yourself.)
  • Learn to be Self-Dependant(Aatmanirbhar)
  • Become a businessman/businesswoman without an office.
