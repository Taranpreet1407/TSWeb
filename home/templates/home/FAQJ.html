Frequently Asked Questions

What is the need of career counseling session?

Due to pandemic there has been a confusion which is arising between the Job seekers and the corporate companies. Even the corporate policies have changed. Due to this we have taken this initiative that the job seekers will go through our counseling session which would clear their doubts which would help them in their interview as well.

The salary will be decided by the company, the post, the CGPA, the qualifications and the position you have been selected for. Looking forward we are expecting a minimum salary of 2.4 Lakh per annum to 6 Lacs per annum.

Is the Job work from home?

It always depend on the company weather it has WFH options for the freshmen candidates or not. It even depends on the state government laws too.

We have an escaltion chatbot where you can address all your doubts. We will respond you as soon as possible.

TalentServe has a strict disciplinary policy where we do not entertain any indiscipline either from our employees or from interns. Strict actions will be taken on an immediately.

When will the job seekers attend the career counseling session?

As soon and the Job Seeker registers , he/she will get an automated email from our side regarding their session date and time within 5-7 days. After the session is over within a week they will start getting calls for interviews.

Any Jobseeker/ Students who is of an age 18+ with 0-2 Years Experience can apply. The Jobseeker / Students Can be MBA( Pass out or Pursuing), Engineering( Pass out or Pursuing), UG eg.BA, BCOM, BBA , BMM, BMS , Intermediate passouts, 12th passouts etc(Pass out or Pursuing) can enroll for this session.

Yes! The students who are not looking for jobs are eligible for part time jobs as well as internships in various companies. These jobs will be assured paid.
